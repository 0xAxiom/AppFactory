## Market Trends

### 1. Subscription Fatigue (High Opportunity)
Consumers underestimate subscription spending by $133/month on average, creating demand for tracking and cancellation tools.

**Evidence**: C+R Research 2024 found actual spend of $219 vs perceived $86. Rocket Money users report $200+ savings in first week.

### 2. Simplicity Demand (High Opportunity)
Users abandoning complex productivity apps for simpler alternatives, citing 'spending more time organizing than doing.'

**Evidence**: Reddit r/productivity threads, Android Authority reviews citing Notion slowness and feature bloat.

### 3. Habit Tracker Retention Crisis (Medium Opportunity)
Habit apps see sharp decline after 2-3 weeks due to lack of meaningful behavioral feedback.

**Evidence**: Straits Research market analysis, user complaints about gamification fatigue and wrong system fit.

### 4. Home Asset Management Gap (High Opportunity)
Households losing $400+/year on expired warranties and unprovable claims.

**Evidence**: SlipCrate research, NerdWallet guides on home inventory needs.
