# Product Specification: Simple Habit Dots

**App Concept**: Visual habit tracking, no limits  
**Category**: Productivity/Health & Fitness  
**Target Launch**: 2026 Q2

## Core Value Proposition

Unlimited visual habit tracking using colored dots without artificial restrictions or complex features.

## Target Users

### Primary Segment: Habit Tracking Enthusiasts
**Pain Points**:
- Artificial limits on number of habits in free versions
- Complex gamification and RPG elements getting overwhelming  
- Data syncing problems and widget failures
- Premium subscription required for basic unlimited tracking

**Goals**:
- Track multiple habits without paying monthly fees
- Simple visual progress tracking
- Reliable data saving without sync issues
- Clean interface without overwhelming features

**Demographics**: Adults 25-45 focused on self-improvement and building consistent routines

### Secondary Segment: Neurodivergent Users
**Pain Points**:
- Rigid daily completion requirements
- Habits tied to specific days rather than flexible schedules
- Complex interfaces with steep learning curves

**Goals**:
- Flexible habit scheduling that accommodates varying routines
- Simple visual feedback without pressure
- Offline-first tracking without internet dependency

## Core Features (MVP)

### 1. Unlimited Habit Creation
Create and track unlimited habits without restrictions. Users can add as many habits as needed without hitting artificial limits or upgrade prompts.

### 2. Visual Dot Calendar  
Calendar view showing colored dots for habit completion with monthly and yearly views available. Clear visual patterns show streaks and gaps.

### 3. Simple Tap Tracking
One-tap habit completion marking with visual feedback and undo capability for accidental taps.

### 4. Streak Visualization
Simple streak counter showing current and best streaks with milestone celebrations without overwhelming gamification.

### 5. Offline-First Data Storage
All features work without internet connection with data persisting locally on device, eliminating sync failures.

## Premium Features

### Pattern Analytics
Insights into habit patterns and correlations providing ongoing value for users wanting deeper insights.

### Data Export
Export habit data to CSV or other formats for power users needing data portability.

### Advanced Visualizations
Heat maps, trend lines, and correlation charts providing value beyond basic dot tracking.

### Backup and Sync
Cloud backup and cross-device synchronization for data security and multi-device access.

## Success Metrics

### User Engagement
- **Daily Active Users**: 40%+ DAU/MAU ratio reflecting daily habit tracking behavior
- **Session Length**: 2-3 minutes average (quick check-in pattern)  
- **Retention Rate**: 70%+ 7-day retention, 45%+ 30-day retention

### Business Metrics
- **Conversion to Paid**: 15-20% conversion rate after 30 days (analytics features)
- **Monthly Revenue Per User**: $1.50-2.50 ARPU with $1.99-3.99 subscription
- **Churn Rate**: 5-8% monthly churn (low due to forever-free core features)

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors

**HabitBull**: Free version limited to 5 habits, premium required for unlimited  
**Differentiation**: We offer unlimited habits for free with simpler interface

**Habitify**: Feature-rich but complex, free version restrictions  
**Differentiation**: We focus on simplicity over feature complexity

**Loop Habit Tracker**: Free unlimited habits, no ads (Android only)  
**Differentiation**: We bring Loop's simplicity to iOS with strategic premium features  

**Streaks (iOS)**: Beautiful design, limited to 24 habits, $4.99 upfront  
**Differentiation**: We offer unlimited habits with freemium model

## Market Positioning

The only habit tracker that combines unlimited free tracking with a simple visual interface, addressing the core frustrations users have with artificial limits and complex features.
