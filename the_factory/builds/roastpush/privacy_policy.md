# Privacy Policy for RoastPush

**Last Updated: January 12, 2026**

## Overview

RoastPush ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

RoastPush is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data:

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

- **Settings Preferences**: Your intensity level, notification schedule, and category selections are stored locally on your device using secure storage.

- **Roast History**: The insults you receive are stored locally on your device for your viewing in the history feature.

- **Onboarding Status**: A simple flag indicating whether you've completed onboarding.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect your name, email, or personal identifiers
- We do NOT track your location
- We do NOT access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
- We do NOT sell or share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

### RevenueCat
For subscription management, we use RevenueCat. When you make a purchase:
- RevenueCat receives your anonymous App Store/Play Store user ID
- RevenueCat processes your subscription status
- We receive only your subscription status (premium or not)
- We do NOT receive your payment information

For RevenueCat's privacy practices, see: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

### Apple App Store / Google Play Store
For payment processing:
- All payment information is handled entirely by Apple or Google
- We never see or store your credit card or payment details
- Purchases are tied to your App Store/Play Store account

## Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your device using:
- SQLite database for roast history
- AsyncStorage for preferences

When you delete the app, all local data is permanently removed from your device.

## Push Notifications

The core functionality of RoastPush requires local push notifications:
- Notifications are scheduled locally on your device
- No server communication is required for notifications
- Notification content is generated from locally stored insult data
- You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- All data stays on your device
- No network transmission of personal data
- No cloud storage or synchronization

## Children's Privacy

RoastPush is intended for users 17 years of age and older due to mature humor content. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 17. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- Disable notifications through device settings
- Request information about your data (contact us)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

**Email**: support@roastpush.app

## Legal Basis for Processing

For users in the EU/EEA, our legal basis for processing is:
- Legitimate interest in providing app functionality
- Contract performance for subscription services

## Data Retention

- Local data is retained until you delete the app
- Subscription data is retained by RevenueCat per their policies
- We do not retain any data on our servers
