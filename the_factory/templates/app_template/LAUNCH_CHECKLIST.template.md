## Common Rejection Reasons

### iOS
- **Crashes or bugs** - Test thoroughly before submission
- **Incomplete metadata** - Fill in all required fields
- **Placeholder content** - Remove all lorem ipsum and test data
- **Privacy issues** - Accurately describe data collection
- **Payments outside IAP** - All digital purchases must use IAP

### Android
- **Policy violations** - Review Google Play policies
- **Crashes** - Check crash reports in Play Console
- **Deceptive behavior** - App must do what it says
- **User data** - Accurately complete data safety form
