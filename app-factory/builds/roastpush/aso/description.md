# RoastPush - App Store Description

**Get roasted when you least expect it.**

RoastPush sends you random, hilarious insults throughout your day via push notifications. No effort required—just turn it on and let the roasts fly.

## Why RoastPush?

Ever wish someone would just randomly insult you during your boring Tuesday afternoon? No? Well, too bad—RoastPush does it anyway.

We deliver surprise roasts straight to your lock screen at completely random times. You never know when the next burn is coming. That's the fun part.

## Features

**Surprise Attacks**
Get insulted at random times throughout your day. During your morning coffee. In the middle of a meeting. Right before bed. We don't discriminate.

**Choose Your Pain Level**
- Mild Tease: Gentle jabs for the sensitive souls
- Solid Roast: The sweet spot of insults
- Savage Burns: For those who can truly handle it (Premium)

**Control Your Schedule**
Set your active hours so you don't get roasted at 3 AM (unless you're into that). Choose how many daily roasts you can handle.

**Pick Your Categories**
Get roasted about work, dating, fitness, intelligence, or just general life failures. Mix and match to personalize your suffering.

**Roast History**
Relive your favorite burns anytime. Save the best ones to share with friends who need to be humbled.

## Perfect For

- People with thick skin and a sense of humor
- Anyone who thinks they're too confident
- Fans of roast battles and comedy
- Those who appreciate a good insult
- People whose day needs random chaos

## Free Features

- 5 daily roasts

## Privacy First

All your roasts stay on your device. We don't track you, sell your data, or judge you (that's the app's job). Completely offline capable.

## Warning

This app WILL hurt your feelings. That's literally the point. Download only if you can take a joke. Actually, download even if you can't—that's even funnier.
