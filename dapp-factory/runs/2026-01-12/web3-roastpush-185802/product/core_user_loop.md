## Session Flow

### 1. Quick Match (Casual)
- Connect wallet
- Tap "Quick Match"
- Wait <30 seconds for opponent
- 3 rounds, 30 seconds each
- Win/lose, no stakes
- Get clip, share optionally

### 2. Ranked Battle (Competitive)
- Connect wallet with ROAST balance
- Select entry tier (10/50/100 ROAST)
- Matched with similar skill rating
- Best of 3 or 5 rounds
- Winner takes pool minus 5% platform fee
- Leaderboard points awarded

### 3. Group Battle (Social)
- Create or join 3-8 player room
- Host sets theme and entry fee
- Tournament bracket or free-for-all
- Progressive elimination
- Final winner takes majority pot

## Engagement Mechanics

### Short-Term (Session)
- **Matchmaking Speed**: <30s wait time
- **Round Tension**: 15-45s countdown pressure
- **Live Reactions**: Emoji feedback during delivery
- **Instant Results**: AI scoring with funny commentary

### Medium-Term (Daily)
- **Daily Challenges**: Themed prompts with bonus rewards
- **Streak Bonuses**: Consecutive day participation rewards
- **Leaderboard Climb**: Daily reset creates fresh competition
- **Clip Highlights**: Best moments pushed to feed

### Long-Term (Weekly/Monthly)
- **Weekly Tournaments**: Larger prize pools
- **Season Rankings**: Tier promotions/demotions
- **Achievement System**: Unlock cosmetics through milestones
- **Reputation Building**: Verified skill rating

## Token Integration Points

| Action | Token Flow |
|--------|------------|
| Enter ranked battle | User pays entry fee |
| Win battle | User receives prize pool |
| Tip roaster | Viewer tips performer |
| Buy cosmetic | User purchases from shop |
| Stake for leaderboard | Lock tokens for eligibility |
| Platform fee | 5% of prize pools |

## Retention Hooks

1. **Loss Aversion**: Entry fee creates stakes
2. **Social Proof**: Leaderboard visibility
3. **Content Creation**: Clips for social media
4. **Skill Progression**: Rating improvement
5. **Token Appreciation**: Ecosystem growth benefits
