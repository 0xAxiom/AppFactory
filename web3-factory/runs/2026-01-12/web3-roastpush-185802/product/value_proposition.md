# RoastPush Value Proposition

## Core Value

Turn your roasting skills into verifiable earnings through competitive voice-first battles with AI judging and instant rewards.

## For Roasters (Competitors)

- **Monetize Your Wit**: Earn ROAST tokens from battle victories
- **Build Reputation**: Climb verifiable onchain leaderboards
- **Go Viral**: Instant clip generation for social sharing
- **Global Competition**: Compete against anyone, anywhere
- **Instant Payouts**: No waiting for prize distribution

## For Viewers (Audience)

- **Watch Epic Battles**: Real-time roast competitions
- **React Live**: Emoji reactions influence scoring
- **Tip Favorites**: Support roasters with micro-tips
- **Discover Talent**: Find the funniest creators globally

## For Creators (Power Users)

- **Host Themed Rooms**: Create custom battle arenas
- **Build Following**: Grow audience through victories
- **Earn Passively**: Receive tips from fans
- **Premium Content**: Access exclusive themes/cosmetics

## Competitive Advantages Over Web2

| Feature | Web2 Alternative | RoastPush |
|---------|------------------|-----------|
| Prize Distribution | Delayed, opaque | Instant, transparent |
| Leaderboards | Manipulable | Onchain, verifiable |
| Global Access | KYC required | Permissionless |
| Micro-tips | 30%+ fees | Near-zero fees |
| Prize Pools | Platform controlled | Smart contract held |

## Why Now

1. **Gen-Z demands authenticity**: Skill-based competition over algorithm luck
2. **Creator economy maturation**: Direct monetization without platform cuts
3. **Solana maturity**: Fast, cheap transactions enable real-time gaming
4. **Social entertainment growth**: Short-form competitive content trending
