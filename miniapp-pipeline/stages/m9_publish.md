## How to Publish

Publishing is simple: **create a post containing your app URL**.

### Step 1: Open Base App

Launch the Base app on your mobile device.

### Step 2: Compose a Post

Tap the compose button to create a new post (cast).

### Step 3: Add Your URL

Include your mini app URL in the post:
```
https://[your-project].vercel.app
```

The app will automatically render as a rich embed when posted.

### Step 4: Add Context

Write a caption that:
- Explains what your app does
- Gives users a reason to try it
- Includes a call to action

### Step 5: Post It

Tap post. Your mini app is now live!

## Caption Best Practices

### DO

**Lead with value:**
> "Track your daily gratitude and see what your friends are thankful for"

**Be specific:**
> "A simple way to share one thing you're grateful for each day"

**Include a call to action:**
> "Try it and share your first gratitude"

**Keep it short:**
1-2 sentences is ideal. Let the app speak for itself.

## Timing Tips

### Best Times to Post
- When your target audience is active
- Avoid posting during major events that dominate the feed
- Consider time zones of your main audience

### Launch Day
- Post once with your main announcement
- Engage with replies and comments
- Don't spam multiple posts

### Follow-Up
- Share user stories (with permission)
- Announce updates and new features
- Re-share occasionally with fresh angles

---

## Post-Launch

### Monitor

**Check for issues:**
- Watch for user reports in comments
- Monitor your app's error logs
- Test the app after posting

**Track engagement:**
- How many people interact with the embed?
- Are users sharing it?
- What feedback are you getting?

### Respond

**Engage with users:**
- Reply to comments
- Thank people for trying it
- Address questions quickly

**Handle issues:**
- Fix bugs promptly
- Communicate about fixes
- Be transparent about problems

### Iterate

**Improve based on feedback:**
- What do users love?
- What's confusing?
- What features are requested?

**Ship updates:**
- Deploy fixes to Vercel
- Manifest updates take up to 24h to propagate
- Announce significant updates

## Growth Strategies

### Organic Growth
- Make sharing easy within the app
- Create shareable moments
- Encourage users to invite friends

### Community Building
- Engage in Base communities
- Collaborate with other builders
- Share your building journey

### Content
- Post about your app regularly (but don't spam)
- Share behind-the-scenes
- Highlight user stories
